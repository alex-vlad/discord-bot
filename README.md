# Discord-Bot

Fun RPG bot with music,a self designed duel system, moderator and plenty other commands.

**Fun**:

 * *roll* - rolls a random number between 1 and 100
 * *shop* - Items shop
 * *buy* - Buy an item using the id.
 * *remove* - Remove an item from inventory
 * *stats* - Check your balance and inventory
 * *craft* - Craft hero
 * *craft_table* - Crafting requirements
 * *gc* - Transfer coins to another use

**Mod:**
  
 * *ban*        -      Ban a member from the guild
  
 * *kick*          -   Kick a member from the guild
  
 * *purge*       -     Delete messages a certain number of messages from a channel.
  
 * *softban*      -    Kicks a members and deletes their messages.
 
 * *unban*      -    Unban a member from the guild
  
 * *warn*       -      Warn a member via DMs

**Music:**
  
 * *join*        -     Joins a channel
  
*  *leave*        -    Stop the currently playing song and destroy the player.
  
 * *play*         -    Request a song and add it to the queue.
  
 * *repeat*       -    Play a song on repeat
  
 * *shuffle*      -    Shuffles the queue.
  
*  *skip*        -     Skip the song.
